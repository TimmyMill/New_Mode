Your Private Data Might Not Be As Secure As You Think

#}

Modern mobile devices have become so sophisticated that they're basically smaller versions of computers, making them susceptible to the same security risks. The CompTIA conducted a survey of more than 2,000 people and found that viruses and worms, which affected 54% of users, and spyware, which affected 51%, continue to be the top two security threats. (Mobile Device Security Is A Concern). Based on a 2015 survey by the Pew Research Center, 68% of adults in the US own smartphones. (Dai et al.) With this number having likely risen, mobile device security impacts a large number of Americans.

As we continue to make advancements in technology, the capabilities of mobile devices increase as well. Here are just a few of the most common smart phone uses:

  • Texting
  • Social Media
  • Entertainment Streaming
    • Music
    • Movies
    • TV
  • Camera
  • Weather
  • Navigation
  • Online Shopping
